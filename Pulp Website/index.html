Pulp Collective aims to provide a platform to amplify the voices of Indigenous creatives. We want to have open conversations and share the work of Uni students, to celebrate their unique skills and stories within our creative community.

As young design students ourselves, we can empathise with how inaccessible and unattainable creative spaces can be. We are passionate about social change, identifying issues around accessibility and inclusivity within the creative community.
